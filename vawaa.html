Full customer pipeline · Cold → Warm → Hot · Managed end-to-end

Managed VAWAA's entire customer pipeline — a platform connecting travelers with artist residencies worldwide — from cold lead generation through to conversion and retention. Built internal SOPs for the growth team, designed multi-stage email nurture sequences, and sustained consistent quarterly performance across a long sales cycle that measured success in months, not days.
